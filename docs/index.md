# GroundTruth

**Advisor-grade UK financial planning — without the £1,500 fee.**

GroundTruth is a computational financial planning platform built for self-directed UK professionals. It runs the same multi-stage analysis a chartered financial planner would walk you through — tax, cashflow, debt, pensions, mortgage, estate — and returns a complete report in seconds.

## Who it's for

- UK earners making £40k-£200k who want to make deliberate, informed financial decisions
- People comfortable entering their own numbers and interpreting analysis
- Self-employed professionals who don't fit neatly into off-the-shelf advice models
- Anyone who has read "The Barefoot Investor", "Millionaire Next Door", or "Your Money or Your Life" and wants the analysis to match the ambition

## What it does

You supply a financial profile (income, expenses, debts, savings, goals). GroundTruth runs it through 22 calculation modules and produces:

- A **0-100 health score** across seven weighted categories, with grade
- **Priority actions** ranked by impact
- **Lifetime cashflow** projected year-by-year through retirement
- **Monte Carlo** investment projections with percentile bands
- **Tax optimisation** across income, pension, ISA, LISA, CGT, IHT
- **Stress scenarios** — job loss, rate shock, market drawdown, compound trees
- **Mortgage readiness** with LTV bands, overpayment modelling, stress tests
- **Estate planning** — IHT liability, gift strategies, taper relief, RNRB taper
- **PDF/CSV/XLSX exports** for record-keeping or sharing with an IFA

## What it is not

GroundTruth is an **information service**, not a regulated financial adviser. It provides computational analysis of data you supply — not personal recommendations. It is not authorised by the FCA to give advice. For regulated advice, consult a qualified IFA.

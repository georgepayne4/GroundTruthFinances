# Life Events

This page is the year-by-year view of your financial life — wealth trajectory, milestones, and scheduled changes.

## The timeline

A year-by-year projection starting from today, with one row per year showing:

- **Age** — yours
- **Events** — any scheduled life events for that year
- **Gross income** — projected income including planned changes
- **Net income** — after tax/NI
- **Expenses** — projected spending
- **Debt payments** — annual servicing
- **Annual surplus** — income minus spending
- **Savings rate** — that year's rate
- **Liquid savings** — projected cash/ISA balance
- **Investments** — pension and GIA balances
- **Total debt** — remaining obligations
- **Net worth** — assets minus debt
- **Property value / mortgage / equity** — if applicable

## Scheduled life events

Your profile's `life_events` section lets you schedule:

- **Income changes** — job change, promotion, sabbatical, retirement
- **Expense changes** — kids (education/childcare), downsizing, care costs
- **One-off events** — inheritance, property purchase, relocation

Each event has a year offset. The projection applies changes at the right year, compounds forward, and shows the aggregate effect on net worth.

## Milestones

The dashboard surfaces automatic milestones:

- **Debt-free** — year your last non-mortgage debt clears
- **Mortgage-free** — year you own the property outright
- **Financial independence (25× expenses)** — when investments cover 4% of your spending indefinitely
- **Retirement-ready** — when projected pension meets adequacy threshold
- **State pension age** — fixed by DOB

Milestones give you a visual map of *when* you hit key life stages — not just whether you'll hit them.

## How to use this page

1. **Sanity check the trajectory.** Does net worth grow, flatten, or decline? If declining, why?
2. **Identify inflection points.** Big events (kids, house, retirement) show as visible bends in the curve.
3. **Run what-ifs.** Add a life event (early retirement at 55) and re-analyse. Compare.
4. **Check retirement readiness.** The projection carries forward through retirement, showing whether your pot lasts.

## What life events does not show

- **Probability-weighted outcomes** — this is deterministic. For uncertainty bands, see the [Scenarios](scenarios.md) page or enable Monte Carlo.
- **Tax year transitions** — bands are projected forward using current rules. If future chancellors change tax, projections drift.
